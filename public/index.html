 Platform features

Everything your academic life needs

Campus Connect brings together all the tools students, teachers, and administrators use daily into one clean, fast experience.

Attendance Tracking

Teachers mark attendance per subject and students see their real-time attendance percentage and monthly history at a glance.

Performance & Grades

Internal marks, semester results, cultural achievements, and teacher remarks — all in one structured view for every student.

Subject Resources

Access notes, previous year question papers, and live class links organized neatly by department and semester.

Academic Calendar

Stay on top of exams, holidays, and college events with a color-coded monthly calendar everyone can see.

Departments & Semesters

Browse all departments, drill into any semester, and find the subjects and resources you need in seconds.

College Guidelines

Policies, rules, and important notices published by the administration, always current and easy to find.
